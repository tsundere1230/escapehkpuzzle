Ahh... how you missed the night sky, the stars, and the constellations... here you'd think there would be no stars, but at this close to the edge of the land, the light pollution is really a lot milder than you think. It's not quite the starry sky you see from photos, and of course, the milky way isn't visible. You have no regrets making it all the way here though, this is surely better than anything you get in the city center.
