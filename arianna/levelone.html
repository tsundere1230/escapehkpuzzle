Most of the time, you'll want to solve the riddle by changing the url. Currently it is ' ... /arianna/levelone.html'.

As part of the solution, you should only ever use the lower case a-z, 0-9, and slashes (you can ignore them for now, more on slashes on level 8).

 

The 'arianna' part is the folder and 'levelone' is the url of the level. You can ignore the actual name of the folder. It could have said 'aaron' for example, and function the same way. Each district of the real map of HK has its own folder, mostly. I say mostly because certain, more crowded districts will get multiple folders. 'Arianna' is technically the tutorial folder, and "Shatin" district (the district you're in now) is the B folder.) It goes without saying that you don't *need* any prior knowledge of anything, although it could certainly help in some situations.

 

Anyways, to solve the riddle, you will want to check everything in a level. Granted, this is easier said than done.
